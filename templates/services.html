Type of Therapies

  • Individual
  • Couples
  • Teen/child

Individual Therapy

In this session you will experience one-on-one mental health treatment that is personalized to suit an individual's unique needs. It involves setting therapeutic goals, processes one's past, and learning how to manage one's symptoms or triggers in order to live a healthier life. It ensures confidentiality,provides a comfortable environment,confirms focused attention,allows flexibility,assures a comprehensive treatment,promotes a healthy relationship with the Counsellor,enables a better communication and reduces the possibility of personal conflicts.

Couples Therapy

This is usually short-term counseling designed to help couples understand and resolve problems, dissatisfaction, and conflict in their relationship/marriage.

Teen Therapy
